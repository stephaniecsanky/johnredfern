About

John Redfern is a painter originating from Brantford, Ontario. Insert bio information and some introduction/backstory.

John has had experience painting and studying with painters such as Michael Roth and Peter Etril Snyder. He paints a variety of things, from portraits to wildlife and landscapes. John also has experience working in a variety of mediums including pastel, oil and acryllic.
